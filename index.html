Jeju Agricultural School / Korea Land & Housing Corperation

One of the early centers of higher education on Jeju Island, where the student movement against the US military occupation was intense. Many local residents, including the students and faculty members of the school, were held prisoners and eventually executed/

Hanmosal / Pyoseon Library

Hanmosal was a site of mass killing by the army. Once, 200 civilians were massacred in a week.

Mosulbong Ammo Depot / Daejeong High School

Japanese-built ammo depot in the Daejung area, which turned into a site of massacre after Japanese army leaved the island. Once, 78 captured residents from nearby Hallim, Hankyung, Daejeong, and Andeok area were massacred in a single day

Jocheon Middle School / Jocheon Public Medical Center

Jocheon Middle School, established in 1946, consisted of about 200 students. In 1948, one student was killed while getting tortured by the police, and the school was soon disestablished by the government, after the government implemented the so-called “eradication campaign”.

Hagwi Middle School (2nd year classroom) / Church

Hagwi middle school students were often detained in Jeju police stations accused of being left-wing activists. Some students were arrested, tortured, or even killed solely because they were students of Hagwi Middle School.

Hagwi Middle School (1st year classroom) / Parking Lot

Hagwi middle school was closed six months after the April 3rd incident, due to government oppression, in October 1948.

Mosulpo Police Station / Hotel

Mosulpo police station is known as the center of the Korean army which led the massacre in Jeju island. On March 14th, 1948, about twenty days before the April 3rd incident, one young adult ended up being killed while getting tortured in Mosulpo police station. This sparked the anger of Jeju civilians, building up to the April 3rd raids on police stations.

Daejeong Police Station / Orchard

Daejeong police station was raided in the April Third uprising by a left wing partisan group, leaving one police officer dead. Also, on May 20th of the same year, 41 deserters from the Korean army once again attacked and burnt down the police office, killing 5 and injuring 2 police officers.
